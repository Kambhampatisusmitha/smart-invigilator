Our Services

Real-Time Monitoring

Streams live video from surveillance cameras in the exam hall to invigilators or a control room. Highlights any unusual movements or behaviors, like students leaving their seats or using unauthorized devices.

Alert System

Real-time Notifications: Sends alerts to invigilators about unusual activities or rule violations, such as unusual behaviors (e.g., multiple head turns). Provides pop-ups, sounds, or color-coded flags on the monitoring dashboard. Uses AI or predefined rules to detect specific events.

Exam Schedule

Allows setting exam dates, times, and durations for each subject or batch. Assigns exam halls based on availability and capacity. Sends reminders or updates to students and invigilators about the schedule.

Emotion Detection

Identifies emotions like anxiety, frustration, or calmness by analyzing facial cues. Detects potential cheating or discomfort based on emotional variations. Triggers notifications for invigilators when unusual emotional patterns are detected.

Gesture Detection

Detects hand gestures like writing, hiding, or using unauthorized devices. Differentiates between normal actions (writing, raising a hand) and abnormal ones (unauthorized signaling).
